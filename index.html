White is the color of elegance in its purest form, a blank page on which the world’s greatest stories are written. It evokes a sense of serenity, purity, and simplicity, yet carries an undercurrent of luxury and sophistication. Like a soft whisper on a snowy winter morning, white is both quiet and powerful. It reflects light, making spaces feel more expansive, and embodies the timeless allure of innocence and fresh beginnings.
